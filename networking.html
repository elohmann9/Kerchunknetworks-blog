Why This Architecture Works

Our fire engine network design provides several critical advantages:

  • Redundancy: Dual WAN connections ensure connectivity even when cellular networks are congested or unavailable
  • Reliability: Enterprise-grade equipment designed for 24/7 operation in harsh environments
  • Performance: Optimized routing and QoS ensures critical applications always have priority
  • Management: Cloud-based monitoring and configuration enables fleet-wide updates and troubleshooting
  • Security: Multiple layers of protection including firewalls, encryption, and access controls
  • Scalability: Architecture supports additional devices and services as needs evolve
